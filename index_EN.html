Travel tips

The wedding will take place at Daniela’s family private garden, away from the buzz of the city. It is located approximately 30 mins from the center of Quito. The best way to reach is by car (we suggest Uber).

The closest airport is Quito International Airport (UIO). You can check the best travel options using skyscanner.com.

In Quito, the best neighbourhoods to stay are:
  • Iñaquito, if you want to stay in a modern and vibrant neighbourhood, close to the city’s largest park, restaurants, and shopping malls.
  • Gonzalez Suarez, a modern residential neighborhood known for its modern architecture, stunning city views, and proximity to parks and cafes. Here we have had good experiences with the following hotels: Stubel and Hotel Quito.
  • La Floresta, an alternative bohemian neighborhood filled with colorful murals, artistic cafes, and a lively cultural scene.
  • Cumbayá, a cute suburb in the Quito valley, with cozy cafes, parks, shops and restaurants; also, the closest to the wedding venue. Here you will have the best experience by renting a full house (for example using airbnb or booking)
